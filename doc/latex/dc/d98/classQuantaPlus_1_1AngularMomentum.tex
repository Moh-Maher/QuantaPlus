\mbox{\hyperlink{classQuantaPlus_1_1AngularMomentum}{Angular\+Momentum}} is a publicly derived class from QM\+\_\+operator$<$\+T$>$, where eigenkets and operators will be represented by column vectors and square matrices, respectively. The matrices representing $J^2$ and $J_z$ in the $j,m$ eigenbasis are diagonal, their diagonal elements being equal to ( after set $\hbar=1$) $j(j+1)$ and $m$, respectively
