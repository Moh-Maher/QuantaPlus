\subsubsection*{template$<$class T$>$\newline
class Quanta\+Plus\+::\+Bra$<$ T $>$}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classQuantaPlus_1_1Bra}{Bra}} is a publicly derived class from the well designed Eigen\+::\+Matrix, where the \mbox{\hyperlink{classQuantaPlus_1_1Bra}{Bra}} vectors are matrices with one row, and Dynamic-\/size column. 

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
While Kets belong to the Hilbert space $H$, the corresponding Bras belong to its dual (Hilbert) space $H_d$. 
\end{DoxyNote}
