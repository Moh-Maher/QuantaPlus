Detailed Description

template<class T>
class QuantaPlus::Bra< T >

Bra is a publicly derived class from the well designed Eigen::Matrix, where the Bra vectors are matrices with one row, and Dynamic-size column.

while kets belong to the Hilbert space H, the corresponding bras belong to its dual (Hilbert) space.
